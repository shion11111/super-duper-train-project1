Nuremberg, Germany

Experience History, Culture, and Flavor

Things To Do

  • Explore the historic city of Nuremberg and its medieval architecture.
  • Walk through a lively outdoor market and enjoy local crafts and food.
  • Watch a beautiful performance at a traditional ballet theater.
  • Taste authentic German bratwurst from a local street vendor.
  • Try the famous spaghetti ice cream, a fun and unique German dessert.
